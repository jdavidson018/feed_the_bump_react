## Featured Recipe

### Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick, nutritious meal packed with protein and vegetables.

#### Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic, minced
- Soy sauce to taste

#### Instructions

1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
2. Heat oil in a large pan and add minced garlic.
3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
4. Mix in cooked quinoa and season with soy sauce.
